Who Am I?
Hi! My name is Amelia Quane and I am an energetic, caring and responsible dog walker. I am a native Chicagoan and have been a professional dog walker for the past year and a dog owner for over 10 years. I know how to keep your dog safe, happy and healthy while we walk through your Chicago neighboorhood. I truly believe that a happy dog is a walked dog and I cannot wait to connect with your canine and give them the outdoor exercise they need and deserve. I canalso run or jog with your dog. I am experienced in walking all breeds of dogs in all types of weather and am highly aware of the warning signs extreem weather may cause.
